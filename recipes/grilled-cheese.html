Grilled Cheese:

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 tablespoons butter (30 g), cut into 3 even pieces
    • 2 slices hearty white bread
    • 2 slices American, cheddar, or Jack cheese
    • Kosher salt

  • Directions:

    1. Melt 1 piece of butter in large nonstick skillet. Add both piece of bread and cook until golden brown.

    2. Transfer bread to cutting board, toasted side up. Place slices of cheese on top of one slice, close sandwhich, bit both toasted sides facing inwards.

    3. Melt 1 piece of butter on skillet, medium-low. Add sandwhich, swirling occasionally, until deep and golden brown. Remove sandwhich place last piece of butter and put sandwhich in cooked side up.
